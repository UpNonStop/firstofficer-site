Employee cards count against 5/24 for Chase applications. The Strategist tracks every team member and identifies windows for high-value signup bonuses like the Ink Preferred at 100K UR.

Your $14K per month in Google Ads earns 3x on Ink Preferred (MCC 7311). Your $3K in SaaS earns 3x. But your $8K in miscellaneous earns 1x on a generic card. The Strategist splits spend to capture every multiplier.

Annual Fee ROI Analysis

Is the $695 Amex Platinum paying for itself through the airline credit, Uber credit, and lounge access for your 3 traveling team members? The Strategist calculates the net return on every card fee and recommends keeps, cancels, and downgrades.

Signup Bonus Sequencing

Ink Preferred first (100K UR, 3x on ads). Then Amex Business Gold (4x on top 2 auto-selected business categories). Then Blue Business Plus (2x on everything, no annual fee). The order matters. The Strategist builds the 12-month application calendar.
