The mistakes

What Veterinary Clinics Get Wrong

These aren't obvious. Nobody in the veterinary clinics industry talks about points optimization. Here's what we see over and over.

01

MWI and Covetrus orders on debit or a basic card

Veterinary supply distributors code as wholesale or medical supply. no bonus category on any card. But the right catch-all card earns 2x on everything. On $8K/month, that's 192,000 points per year instead of zero on debit.

The math

$8K/month supplies on the right catch-all card = 192K pts/year at 2x.

02

Not routing marketing spend to a bonus card

Google Ads for new client acquisition earn 3x on the right business card. Even $1,500/month earns 54,000 points per year on the right card.

The math

$1.5K/month ads at 3x = 54K pts/year.

03

Practice management software on any random card

Cornerstone, Avimark, and eVetPractice may code as software subscriptions on Amex. earning 4x on the right business card. Not guaranteed, but worth testing with a small purchase.

The math

$800/month software at 4x = 38,400 pts/year.

04

Missing out on business card signup bonuses entirely

Two business cards in year one yield 170K+ bonus points worth $2,500+ in travel. Most veterinarians don't even know business credit cards are a separate category from personal cards.

The math

Two business cards = 170K+ bonus points = $2,500 to $3,500 in travel.

How it works

Your vendors. Our routing.

Every vendor sends a merchant category code (MCC) when you swipe. That code determines your multiplier. The same $5,000 payment earns 5,000 points on the wrong card or 20,000 on the right one. We match every vendor to its highest-earning card.

MCC
Vendor Code Analysis

CAP
Spending Cap Tracking

Every bonus category has annual caps. We shift routing before you hit a ceiling and drop to 1x.

Transfer Partner Math

Earning is half. We route redemptions to partners where points are worth 2x to 5x more than cash.
