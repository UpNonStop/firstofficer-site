What is UpNonStop RoS?

Return on Spend measures the travel value your business earns per dollar of card spend. A standard cashback card returns 1 to 2 cents per dollar. With optimized category routing and transfer partner redemptions, business owners can achieve significantly higher returns. RoS is the single metric that shows the difference.
